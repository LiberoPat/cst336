A Brief History of Agile

Agile is a term which describes series of methods and best practices of software development

The agile methodology is based off of the mission and values from the Agile Manifesto

In the 1990's, specific methodologies pertaining began to rise in popularity, many combined the old

(methods from the 50s) with the new. The methodologies valued face to face interactions with collaborators

and stakeholders. Some Agile concepts include daily meetings, incremental & iterative development,

small teams, milestone retrospect, and user personas based off user experience priniples
